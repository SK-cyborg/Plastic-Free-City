Plastic-Free Living in Cities

The Rise of Smart Cities in a Changing World

By 2050, cities will look drastically different from what we see today. As urban populations continue to grow, the demand for efficient, sustainable, and technologically advanced cities has never been greater. Enter the smart city future, where urban innovation, sustainable tech, and connected infrastructure redefine how people live, work, and interact with their surroundings.

From AI-powered traffic systems to energy-efficient skyscrapers, smart cities will prioritize connectivity, automation, and sustainability. A main part of this transformation is the integration of plastic-free practices into urban planning and daily life.
